Michael K. Williams (1966-2021)

Sadly on the day of this assignment I learned that one of the actors I was most impressed by had passed.

Michael Kenneth Williams (November 22, 1966 – September 6, 2021) was an American actor.

  • He played Omar Little on the HBO drama series The Wire
  • Albert "Chalky" White on the HBO series Boardwalk Empire
  • He was also acclaimed for his role as Jack Gee, husband of Bessie Smith, in the HBO telefilm biopic Bessie.
He acted in supporting roles in a number of films and television series, including The Road, Inherent Vice, The Night Of, Gone Baby Gone, 12 Years a Slave, When We Rise, When They See Us, and Hap and Leonard.

From his wikipedia page


Fellow actor from the HBO show, The Wire, Wendell Pierce had this to say about his coworker.

"If you don’t know, you better ask somebody. His name was Michael K. Williams. He shared with me his secret fears then stepped out into his acting with true courage, acting in the face of fear, not in the absence of it. It took me years to learn what Michael had in abundance."
